THE PROBLEM

Due to deforestation and reduced number of tress polution is increasing to an alarming level. It is not only causing global warming but also ecological imbalance. It is a big threat to nature and mankind.

THE IDEA

A green world cannot be a single person's contribution but a single person's contribution can result into a green healthy world. So we aim to build a community of compassionate, like-minded people where each invidual is a real life hero. The idea is planting and maintaining more and more trees through campaigns in each city and to educate people about forestation.

YOUR ROLE

Join our avenger family. Become a green avenger. Take part in plantation campaigns in your city. Plant a tree, maintain a tree, get scores, win goodies and t-shirt. After a certain minimum score you get to become organiser. Now, you can create campaigns in your city in selected areas and earn point or take part in other campaigns as well. Get exciting ranks and roles.

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN US?

If you are the one who cares about mother nature, you are whole-heartedly welcomed to the green avengers family. Rescue mother earth, become real life avenger.Register for free and become part of a great war.

OUR GOALS

We are a non profit website that aims at planting trees in baren lands and maintaining them till it turns into forest. We aim for a green healthy world. We aim at educating and spreading awareness regarding plantation.
